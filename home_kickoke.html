About Me

Hi, I'm Tina Lüdtke!
My journey as a communicator began in seventh grade when I was writing articles for my high school's website. We were a small group of writers supported by some students that took care of the backend and the CMS. During our sessions, I often wondered about the inner workings of a website. However, I was told to write and I could not do both tech and writing.
Fast forward a few years - Turns out that I can do both. I studied technical writing to nurture my curiosity for tech and learn to communicate effectively. My favorite technical communication topics are content strategy, video documentation, and metadata.
I graduated in December 2020, and continue to learn and grow. Currently, I learn Javascript. I am convinced that my communication skills will benefit from coding, as analytical thinking is the cornerstone to creating audience-appropriate content.
